Vision, Mission, and Core Values
TCSHS
Vision

We dream of Filipinos who passionately love their country and whose competencies and values enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to building the nation. We are learner-centered public institution, the Department of Education continuously improves itself to better serve its stakeholders.

TCSHS
Mission

To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education where:
Students: learn in a child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe, and motivating environment.
Teachers: facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner.
Administrators and staff, as stewards of the institution, ensure an enabling and
supportive environment for effective learning to happen.
Family, community, and other stakeholders: are actively engaged and share
responsibility for developing life-long learners.

About Us
Tagbilaran City Science High School

A particular school dedicated to helping its students improve their approach to problem-solving. Learners are expected to perform well and develop their capacity for critical thought. The school possesses a particular quality that is unique among secondary schools. If a school, whether public or private, satisfies the minimal requirements, it will be regarded as a science high school.

Sir Melchor Daniel created the logo back in 1997. The logo's components each have their own symbolism, which is still valid today. A crucial tool used in all branches of science is the microscope. The globe depicts our planet from space, demonstrating the amount of water and life on earth. The Zigzag line signifies the extension of life by showing the electrocardiogram findings. The boundary that encircles the blue sphere symbolizes the function of science, which is to enclose the planet and prolong its life. Albert Einstein's famous equation, "E=mc2," serves as a reminder to all of us to advance and succeed in our fields like he did in order to uphold his legacy.
